Lights Camera Action (Short Movie)

  1. Film should have a time limit of 5-10 minutes.
  2. Upload your movie on YouTube & submit your video link. If you don’t want your movie to be public, then make it unlisted.
  3. Any obscene and vulgar content is not allowed.
  4. Language is not a barrier. If the language is other than Hindi or English, then subtitles should be used.
  5. A small introduction regarding the theme of the film is expected by the group.
  6. Films should not have any objectionable content or hurt any public sentiments.
  7. At least 2 participants of the group should be present at the time of the screening (provided they are in Mumbai).
  8. A group can submit any number of movies but they will have to register again.

Entry fee: ₹300 (Per Group)

24-hour filmmaking challenge

  1. The theme will be revealed via WhatsApp/SMS at the beginning of 24 hours.
  2. Tasks: Each team has to perform following tasks in the given period of 24 hours Scripting, Casting, Location and Art Design.
  3. Teams can shoot on DSLR or Mobile on 720p or higher. However, the editing must be done on a laptop.
  4. Team Leaders will receive the link for uploading the films at the start of the competition.
  5. Maximum length of film should be 120 seconds including beginning and end credits.
  6. You can have maximum 10 members in your team, excluding the cast.
  7. Any film received after the 24 hour deadline will be disqualified.
